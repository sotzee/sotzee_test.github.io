Short biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) under supervision of Matthew West. I also work with Luke Olson and Scott MacLachlan. The main focus of my Ph.D. has been optimizing partial differential equations solvers using machine learning. Along with my studies at UIUC, I have also done internships at NVIDIA and John Deere where I worked on machine learnign projects, mainly concerning graph neural networks, computer vision, and reinforcement learning. I am originally from Iran and was born and raised in Tehran.
